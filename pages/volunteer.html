Volunteer Opportunities


Friends of Homeless Animals needs volunteers to fulfill our mission of saving abandoned, abused and neglected companion animals. There are many opportunities to volunteer at FOHA. Opportunities include, but aren't limited to, dog walking, cat care and socialization, adoption shift workers, home visits, pet photography, animal bio writers, marketing, advertising, fundraising events, working at the Treasure Hound and much more. Whatever your talent, skill, or desire, we can use your help! If you are interested in volunteering, please fill out the online application below. A Friends representative will be in contact with you soon. Thank you for your interest in FOHA.
